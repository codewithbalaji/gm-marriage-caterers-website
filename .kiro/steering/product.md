# Product Overview

GM Marriage Caterers is a traditional South Indian Brahmin wedding catering service website. The business has 25+ years of experience serving families across Tamil Nadu.

## Core Purpose

Marketing website for a catering business that specializes in:
- Traditional Brahmin wedding catering
- Various auspicious ceremonies (Upanayanam, Seemantham, Grihapravesam, etc.)
- Corporate events with South Indian cuisine

## Key Features

- Bilingual support (English and Tamil)
- Service showcase with 10+ ceremony types
- Menu display with traditional feast items
- Photo gallery of events and food
- Booking/inquiry form
- WhatsApp integration for quick contact

## Target Audience

- Tamil families planning weddings and traditional ceremonies
- Event planners seeking authentic South Indian catering
- Corporate clients needing professional catering services

## Brand Identity

- Traditional, authentic, and trustworthy
- Family-oriented with cultural sensitivity
- Professional service with ritual precision
- 25+ years of heritage and experience
